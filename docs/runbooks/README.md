## What is a Runbook?

A runbook is a concise, step-by-step guide for operators and contributors to handle common
and emergency scenarios. Every runbook follows the structure:

1. **Service overview** — What it does and what it depends on.
2. **Common operations** — Routine maintenance commands.
3. **Alerts and diagnostics** — Symptoms, diagnostic commands, and likely causes.
4. **Escalation path** — Who to contact and how.
5. **Recovery procedures** — Steps to restore normal operation.
